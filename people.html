About the BTC Lab

Led by Prof. Xiaodong Lin and based in the School of Computer Science at the University of Guelph, the BTC Lab focuses on Blockchain technology and Cybersecurity researches.

We use statistics, data science, deep learning and cryptography to gain better understanding of the complexities of cryptocurrency and propose some meaningful schemes related to crybersecurity. Our work has been accepted by a lot of international conferences and journals.

The staff of our lab is mainly from the University of Guelph and the University of Waterloo. We also collaborate with several universities around the world. We are composed of PhD students, post-docs, MSc students and visiting scholars.
